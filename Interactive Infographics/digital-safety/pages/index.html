GUIDE TO THE INTERNET AND DIGITAL SAFETY TIPS FOR PARENTS

The Internet has become an ever-present force in our lives, not just for adults, but for kids as well. Navigating its constant noise, swirl, and pressures present challenges for how parents should educate their children in a competent fashion. By presenting the most up-to-date findings and information on how children and teens are using the Internet now, we hope to empower you to better guide your child in using the Internet, and all that it entails, in a healthy and responsible way.
