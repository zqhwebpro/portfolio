HOW KIDS PRACTICE SAFETY ONLINE

DATA BREACHES

Data breaches can be accidental or
intentional. Always follow up when you
know a data breach has happened and
be sure to all of your passwords.

PHISHING

Phishing is when you are tricked into
giving your information to a fake source.
This can happen from click on links, email,
and opening attachments. Always inspect
all links/emails/attachments before
opening to make sure they are credible.

UNSAFE CONNECTION

Public wifi connections are generally
unsafe. Be sure not to log into accounts,
or make any purchases even on
password protected wifi networks.
