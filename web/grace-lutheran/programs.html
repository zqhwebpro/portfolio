Food Pantry, Sunday School, & TOGs.

Food Pantry

Next Food Distribution: Sept. 30th

Grace Lutheran Church is the home of a food pantry that serves families who reside within the Red Lion Area School District. We routinely distribute food five times a year: September (at the start of school), Thanksgiving, Christmas, Easter and June (at the end of school). In order to qualify to receive food, participants must also meet State Income Guidelines, see chart below.

Here is a documentary on our activities created by the Lower Susquehanna Synod of the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America.

Our pantry relies on funding from the State Food Purchase Program, donations from local organizations and churches and from individual donors. In spite of a recent 20% reduction in county monies, we have still been able to reach out to our regular clients as well as accept new clients throughout the year.

Even though we only distribute at five scheduled dates, emergency food orders consisting of canned goods and dry goods are available anytime by appointment. Because of reduced office hours at the church, you need to reach the Grace Lutheran Food Pantry by calling the following cell phone number: (717) 850-6300. If you do not reach a person, please speak clearly and slowly, leaving your name and a phone number where you can be reached. You will always receive a call back from the Food Pantry.

We started this out-reach ministry over 30 years ago, and through the years it has continued to grow into what you know now as the Grace Lutheran Food Pantry. It is our sincere hope that God will continue to bless us and our church as we strive to do His work—feeding the hungry!

Sunday School

We have been commanded by Jesus to be disciples and teachers of His Word, and we must arm ourselves with knowledge and understanding of that Word. Regardless of age, our faith must be nurtured regularly with Christian education, learning, and fellowship.

The Learning Committee at Grace is committed to providing quality learning experiences for all members. Classes are provided for all ages, as well as a nursery for children younger than two years old.

Our Youth Group: TOG: Tributes of God.

Grace Lutheran's Youth Group has given itself the name T.O.G. (Tributes of God). They chose the name after a popular concept from the book and movie, Hunger Games. What it means for them is that God has chosen each of us for service and to use all of our gifts to give glory to God.
