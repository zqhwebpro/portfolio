About Responsive HTML Emails

Good Design:

It's important in today’s day in age that we web developers keep in mind what to do to make us stand out from the countless advertisements and overloads of information our users can face. That's why we, as Web Designers, take the time, effort, and execution into making a well-thought out responsive HTML5 email. Responsive HTML email also means that it is compatible across all browsers and email services. Each email service is set up slightly different so the only way to show off that well-planned responsive HTML5 email without any errors than there are a few steps into making it displayable across the gamut of browsers and email services with slightly different set-ups.

Precise Code:

Making a email responsive takes some precision. You have to function across multiple devices and your email that you put the effort into designing must show up properly. So when designing keep in mind that things have modernized to keep up with the times. Now designers are able to implement things like fluid grids, images, and media queries to help you out. However, keep it mind: in order to be compatible for all devices, all browsers, and all email clients you will need both good design and precise code.

Make it Responsive:

Also, make it responsive! In order to do so you must be prepared to adjust your layout and styles for device specific needs. Here's what to remember: you can remove the blue from phone numbers on mobile devices with this line of code:

                <meta name="format-detection" content="telephone=no">

The Goal:

By applying the aforementioned principles you ensure that your email never nuisances its receiver. It's important to learn the techniques involved so you don't end up with an HTML email that gets no response.
