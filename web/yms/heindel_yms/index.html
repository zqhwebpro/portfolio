Welcome to York Music Shop Online



Where fair prices and fast service are our priority



York Music Shop is a locally owned and operated establishment offering gear for the beginning to advanced musician. What ever your guitar needs, our staff is here to help you find exactly what you're looking for! We travel throughout the country, around the world, and are in touch with hundreds of sources weekly to provide a constant flow of high quality guitars from investment grade to student models. The student instruments we offer are a great starting value with the opportunity to upgrade by trading in your first instrument. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is available 7 days a week to offer reliable and honest information along with dependable customer service. Our website is updated several times a day and all listings provide accurate and conservative descriptions, high resolution professional grade photographs, and most importantly, prices! We pay MORE $$$$ for fine used gear. We have a no-nonsense, zero-hype approach with fast and professional packing and shipping, as well as objective and stringent quality standards. Please enjoy the site and feel free to call us at 717-793-3458 or contact us online. A short list with some of the new brands instruments and amps we carry include: Martin, PRS, Orange, Peavey, Budda, Vox, Boss, Dunlop, Levy's, Washburn, Marshall, Eden, Randall, Parker, and more! Some of the used equipment we regularly see include: Gibson, Fender, PRS, Gretsch, Rickenbacker, Mosrite, Takamine, Hofner, Dan Electro, Schecter, Epiphone, Taylor, ESP, Teisco, Hamer, Ibanez, Martin, Ovation, Seagull, Mmarginarshall, Orange, Budda, Eden, Crate and many more...
