Weather

With diverse climates spanning the Northeastern states, this region is known for its particularly cold winters and semi-humid summers. Northeastern winters are typically characterized by heavy snowfall and below-freezing temperatures, while summers are on par with the country average if not below in terms of both temperature and humidity.

Visual Feature

Fall Foilage: The Northeastern United States is well known for its colorful fall foliage, which has become a huge draw for tourists and travelers. This is especially true for New York, Pennsylvania, Vermont, and the other New England states.
