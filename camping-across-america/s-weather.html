Weather

Known for its humid, subtropical climate, the southern region of the US experiences hot, humid winters and mild winters (typically). However, the weather can vary drastically from state to state, making camping precautions particular to the area you visit.

Average Tempature Per Season

Winter

30°-55°

Summer

70°-85°

Spring

50°-70°

Fall

50°-75°

Visual Feature

Mountainous, desert-ridden, and filled with a variety of ecosystems and environments, the South is home to a multitude of wildlife and picturesque views.
