West

Including Alaska, Arizona, California, Colorado, Hawaii, Idaho, Montana, New Mexico, Oregon, Utah, Washington, and Wyoming the West consists of a wide variety of geographies; from snow capped mountains, to deserts to the old growth forests of the Pacific Northwest. Because of this diversity, the region has a wide array of flora and fauna making the West home to some of the United States’s best national parks.
