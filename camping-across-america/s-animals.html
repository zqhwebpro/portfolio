Animals

Bobcat

The bobcat and some of its different subspecies can be found throughout the Southern US, particularly in Florida and Texas.

American Alligator

The gators found in Florida, Louisiana, Alabama, Georgia, South Carolina, Mississippi, and Texas prefer cypress swamps, marshes, and freshwater wetlands.

Armadillo

Native to the Americas, the Armadillo and it’s different subspecies can be found in Texas, North Carolina, and Florida.

Wild Boars

Found in Alabama, Arkansas, Florida, Georgia, Louisiana, Mississippi, Texas, and Oklahoma, wild boars are the ancestor of the majority of domestic pig breeds.

Western Diamondback Rattlesnake

This rattlesnake species is responsible for most of the fatal snake bites in the United States and can be found in Texas and areas east of the Mississippi River.

Antelope

Native to many different habitats, the antelope can be found in Texas and Oklahoma. It’s beautiful horns and pelt make it a sight to see in the South.
